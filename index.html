Core Value #1

We aim to foster a vibrant, supportive, and sustainable culture where students of all majors, years, and backgrounds have the opportunity to further personal growth and professional development.

Core Value #2

We proudly represent Stevens on a national scale as an institution of creation, discovery, and leading-edge research that benefits society by enriching the minds of future engineers.

Core Value #3

Thank you to our Sponsors!

Without the generous contributions of the following companies who donated their time, money, or materials, make the continuation of the Stevens SAE Baja team possible. All for which we are extremely grateful.

Benchmark Abrasives

Benchmark Abrasives has more than 20 years experience distributing premium industrial products from around the world to the U.S. industrial market. Using extensive knowledge of the world's best manufacturing facilities, Benchmark selects global suppliers that produce high quality, economically priced products used by professional contractors and individual homeowners.

East Lake Axle

East Lake Axle was created to supply our customers on Ebay and Amazon with high performance/heavy duty axles for off road vehicles at economical prices. East Lake buys in volume direct from cv axle factories around the world and offers them to you at nearly our cost. Their supply chain is second to none, and they pass on the benefits of outstanding quality control, design engineering, and purchasing power.

Ogura Clutch

Ogura has pioneered the use of robotics in electromagnetic clutch and brake manufacturing. We have a dedicated design team responsible for developing continuous innovative ideas to help improve quality and to minimize production costs. Ogura has received the Xerox Award of Excellence. Ogura was also the first company in Japan to achieve the Total Quality Excellence Award (TQE) from Ford Motor Company.

Furick Cup

Furick Cup is a family-owned and operated business that produces some of the highest quality TIG consumables available.

Braddock Metallurgical

Braddock Metallurgical are dedicated to providing our customers with an ongoing exceptional level of heat-treating quality and service.
